Soya Chaap

Description

It is a vegeterian dish popular in Indian sub-continent, it contains of soy protein marinated in a mixture of yogurt and spices, then cooked in traditional clay oven.

Ingredients

  • Thick Curd/Yogurt
  • Red Chilli Powder
  • Turmeric Powder
  • Dried Mango Powder
  • Cumin Powder
  • Soy Proteins(Soya Chaap)
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste

Steps

  1. Soften soya chaap by boiling it in water.
  2. Marinate it in the mixture of curd and spices for 30 minutes.
  3. Cook it in the clay oven for some time.
  4. Squeeze a lemon on it and enjoy.
